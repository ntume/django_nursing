Students

The Career Services Unit offers various FREE career counselling and consulting services to all registered students on all four UJ campuses.
The following services are offered to support and develop you as you embark on your career journey:

Career Assements

…to assist students in their study and career decision making. Feedback discussions are conducted by professionals experienced in career development.

Career Counselling

…focuses on guidance and facilitation in decision making regarding study and career choice. This is often used in combination with career assessment to facilitate a holistic intervention.

Career Advisory and Walk-In Service

…to assist students that arrive without a scheduled appointment. During an advisory session a student is seen by a career consultant for a brief 15 to 20 minutes.

Career Consulting

…that provides students with practical skills for becoming a part of the working world, such as CV writing, job hunting, how to prepare for job interviews and much more!

Workplace-Readiness Workshops

…to equip students with skills for writing CVs and cover letters, interview skills, job searching skills, personal branding tools and much, much more.

Career Resource Centres

…where students may access a variety of printed and electronic career and company information resources.

Graduate Recruitment Programme

…that is well supported by a variety of top national and international companies.

We encourage our students to connect with us − visit our offices, view our website, sign up on the portal, and engage with our online service!

Companies / Employers

We have a variety of services that we will offer companies on the new platform, such as...

Graduate Recruitment Programme

The Recruitment Programme of PsyCaD Career Services aims to facilitate graduate recruitment through dissemination of information and networking opportunities between employers and graduates.

Events / Career Fairs

Career Services features a number of on-campus events and online programmes. This is a great way for students and employers to network, engage and connect, an opportunity to ask questions and for employers to give tips to students seeking to land their next career position.

Job Portal

The Career Services portal is our one-stop graduate-employer career portal. The Career portal creates a platform for students to connect with potential employers in order to address all career-related needs in one, convenient, online location.

Annual Recruitment Guide

The Career Services Recruitment Guide is an annual publication and is created for the purposes of the UJ Recruitment Programme. The guide is jam-packed with various career/job-related resources in order for students to be able to prepare themselves thoroughly for recruitment opportunities within industries.
